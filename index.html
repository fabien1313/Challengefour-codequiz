Highscores

To begin, press the "Start" button and your quiz will begin.
The quiz will consist of 4 questions with 4 multiple choice for you to answer.
You will incur a penalty deduction of 15 seconds when answering a question incorrectly.
When all the questions are answered or you run out of time, the game will end and you will receive your score.
The score will include answers correct plus bonus points for time. Enter your initials and save your score!
